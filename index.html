About Me

I'm Ivy Chisom Adiele Khalid, a Data Analyst and Analytics Engineer based in Lagos, Nigeria, with over 5 years of experience building end to end data systems for businesses. I'm the founder of DatumStack, a data consultancy helping SMEs and startups go from guesswork to clarity.

My work spans the full modern data stack from Python ingestion and SQL transformation to dbt modeling, cloud warehousing on Snowflake, BigQuery, and Redshift, and business intelligence delivery through Power BI and Tableau. I hold an MBA in Business Analytics and a BSc in Management Information Systems from Daystar University.
